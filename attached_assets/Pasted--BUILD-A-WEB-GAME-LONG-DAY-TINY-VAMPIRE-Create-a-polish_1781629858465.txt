# BUILD A WEB GAME: LONG DAY, TINY VAMPIRE

Create a polished browser-based game called "Long Day, Tiny Vampire".

The game should run entirely in the browser using modern web technologies.

Recommended Stack:

* React
* TypeScript
* Vite
* Phaser.js or PixiJS
* TailwindCSS
* Web Audio API

The final game should be deployable on:

* Vercel
* Netlify
* GitHub Pages

The game must work on desktop browsers without requiring downloads.

---

# HIGH CONCEPT

It is the June Solstice.

The longest day of the year.

A tiny vampire wakes up to discover his coffin has accidentally been transported across a country-wide June celebration route.

He must survive the longest day while traveling through multiple June-themed festivals and events before sunset.

His companion is a small bat named NOX who provides hints, comic relief, and occasional protection.

The tone should be:

* Wholesome
* Funny
* Charming
* Emotional
* Family friendly

The game should be memorable and judge-friendly.

---

# PRIMARY DESIGN GOAL

Every level must feel completely different.

DO NOT create five platforming levels.

Instead create five different puzzle/gameplay experiences connected by a shared sunlight survival mechanic.

The player should constantly feel surprised.

---

# CORE MECHANIC

SUNLIGHT MANAGEMENT

The vampire can safely exist in shade.

Sunlight drains energy.

The player must continuously think about where shade exists.

Shade becomes a strategic resource.

---

# UI

Top Left:
Moon-shaped Vampiric Energy Meter

Top Right:
Bat Shield Charges

Top Center:
SOLSTICE SUN TRACKER

This is one of the most important features.

Display a horizontal timeline showing:

SUNRISE → MORNING → NOON → AFTERNOON → SUNSET

A small animated sun moves along the timeline as the player progresses.

Each completed level advances the sun.

This creates constant visual tension.

The player always sees:

"I am racing against the longest day."

As sunset approaches:

* Colors become warmer
* Lighting changes
* Music evolves

---

# COMPANION

NOX THE BAT

Functions:

* Tutorial system
* Hint system
* Storytelling
* Comic relief

Examples:

"Humans really celebrate everything."

"I miss nighttime."

"That giant fish is judging me."

"Have you considered not standing in sunlight?"

---

# HEALTH SYSTEM

VAMPIRIC ENERGY

Standing in sunlight:
Energy drains.

Standing in shade:
Energy regenerates.

Checkpoints:
Restore energy.

If energy reaches zero:

The vampire turns into a pile of ash.

Nox says a random funny line.

Respawn at checkpoint.

---

# SPECIAL ABILITY

BAT SHIELD

Collect Bat Tokens.

A Bat Token grants one shield charge.

Activate to summon a swarm of bats.

Effect:

* Sunlight immunity
* Lasts 5 seconds

Useful for crossing large exposed areas.

---

# LEVEL STRUCTURE

Every level should use a DIFFERENT TYPE OF GAMEPLAY.

---

LEVEL 1

INTERNATIONAL SUSHI DAY

Genre:
Moving Platform Puzzle

Theme:
Massive sushi festival.

Gameplay:

The player rides conveyor belts carrying sushi.

Shade is created by moving serving trays.

The player must time movement using the trays' shadows.

Puzzle Focus:
Movement timing.

Goal:
Escape the sushi district.

---

LEVEL 2

NATIONAL FLIP-FLOP DAY

Genre:
Physics Puzzle

Theme:
Beach festival.

Gameplay:

Giant flip-flops act as catapults.

The player must manipulate flip-flops to redirect themselves across gaps.

Puzzle Focus:
Trajectory and physics.

Goal:
Reach the boardwalk.

---

LEVEL 3

PRIDE PARADE

Genre:
Music Puzzle

Theme:
City-wide celebration.

Gameplay:

Music creates rainbow pathways.

Different musical instruments activate different colored bridges.

The player must activate sounds in the correct order.

Puzzle Focus:
Pattern recognition and rhythm.

This level should feel vibrant and joyful.

Goal:
Cross the parade route.

---

LEVEL 4

ALAN TURING'S GARDEN

Genre:
Logic Puzzle

Theme:
Codebreaking maze.

Gameplay:

The player encounters mechanical cipher machines.

Solve:

* Pattern puzzles
* Symbol matching
* Sequence decoding
* Rotating wheel puzzles

Avoid difficult mathematics.

Focus on intuitive puzzle solving.

Puzzle Focus:
Logic.

Goal:
Escape the maze.

---

LEVEL 5

THE LONGEST AFTERNOON

Genre:
Stealth + Resource Management

Theme:
The sun is strongest.

Gameplay:

Shadows are tiny.

The player must plan routes between small safe zones.

Limited Bat Shields available.

This is the most difficult section.

Puzzle Focus:
Resource management.

Goal:
Survive until evening.

---

FINAL LEVEL

SUNSET

Genre:
Narrative Walk

Theme:
Relief.

Gameplay:

No combat.

No difficult puzzles.

The player simply walks home.

Characters from previous levels appear.

The world feels peaceful.

The player sees the coffin in the distance.

Goal:
Reach home.

---

# SECRET CONTENT

Each level contains:

* Hidden routes
* Secret bat conversations
* Alternate solutions
* Hidden collectibles

At completion:

Display:

PATHS NOT TAKEN

Show:

* Missed secrets
* Hidden routes
* Alternate puzzle solutions
* Unlocked lore

This encourages replayability.

# VISUAL DESIGN

Cute 2D art style.

Storybook aesthetic.

Dynamic lighting.

Sun position visibly changes throughout the game.

The player should physically feel the pressure of the longest day.

# ENDING

The vampire finally reaches his coffin.

Nox lands beside it.

NOX:
"You made it."

VAMPIRE:
"Barely."

The vampire climbs inside.

Nox places a tiny blanket into the coffin.

The lid slowly closes.

Screen fades to black.

Achievement Unlocked:

SURVIVED THE LONGEST DAY

Text appears:

GOODNIGHT.

After a pause:

"The longest day eventually ends."

Roll credits.

The moving sun tracker finally reaches SUNSET and disappears below the horizon.
